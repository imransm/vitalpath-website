Privacy Policy for VitalPath

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

VitalPath ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we access, use, and protect your health data when you use our iOS application ("the App").

Data Access and Storage

Health Data from HealthKit

VitalPath reads health data from Apple's HealthKit framework on your device (with your explicit permission). The app accesses the following types of health data:

  • Sleep Data: Sleep duration, sleep stages (REM, Deep, Light), sleep efficiency, and sleep fragmentation
  • Cardiovascular Data: Heart Rate Variability (HRV), resting heart rate, walking heart rate, and heart rate readings
  • Activity Data: Steps, walking distance, exercise minutes, and flights climbed
  • Body Composition: Weight, BMI, body fat percentage, and lean body mass
  • Energy Data: Basal and active energy burned
  • Vital Signs: Respiratory rate, blood oxygen saturation, body temperature, and VO₂ Max
  • Menstrual Cycle Data: Cycle tracking information (when applicable)
  • Dietary Data: Calorie intake

Important: We only READ health data from HealthKit. We do not write or modify any health data in your Health app.

Data Stays On Your Device: All raw health data accessed from HealthKit remains on your iPhone and is never transmitted off your device unless you explicitly choose to use a cloud-based AI provider (OpenRouter). By default, all processing happens entirely on your device.

How We Use Your Health Data

Your health data is used exclusively on your device to:

  1. Generate personalized health insights and analysis
  2. Display trends and visualizations over time
  3. Provide goal-based recommendations based on your selected health goals
  4. Answer follow-up questions about your health data

Data Storage

  • On Your Device: All raw health data remains on your iPhone and is never transmitted off your device (unless you explicitly opt-in to use OpenRouter)
  • Local Storage: Analysis results and trends are stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your health data on any cloud servers or external databases (except when you explicitly choose OpenRouter)
  • No Data Collection: We do not "collect" data in the traditional sense—we only access data that already exists on your device, and it stays there

AI Processing

VitalPath uses artificial intelligence to analyze your health data. You can choose from multiple AI providers:

1. Apple Intelligence (On-Device Processing)

  • Location: Processing occurs entirely on your device
  • Data Transmission: No data is sent to external servers
  • Requirements: iOS 26+ on supported devices (iPhone 15 Pro or later)
  • Privacy: Maximum privacy - all processing happens locally

2. Local LLM (On-Device Processing)

  • Location: Processing occurs entirely on your device using downloaded models
  • Data Transmission: No data is sent to external servers
  • Privacy: Complete privacy - all processing happens locally
  • Models: Uses llama.cpp (MIT License) for on-device AI inference

3. OpenRouter (Cloud Processing)

  • Location: Processing occurs on OpenRouter's servers
  • Data Transmission: Only aggregated health metrics (averages, summaries) are sent to OpenRouter's API
  • What is Sent:
    • Aggregated metrics (e.g., average sleep duration, average HRV)
    • Your OpenRouter API key (required for API authentication - this is your own API key that you provide)
    • No raw time-series data
    • No personally identifiable information
    • No device identifiers
  • What is NOT Sent:
    • Raw health data points
    • Individual readings or timestamps
    • Personal information or account details (name, email, address, etc.)
    • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • API Key Security:
    • You provide your own OpenRouter API key (not one we generate)
    • The API key is stored securely in iOS Keychain
    • The API key is sent over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
    • The API key is only used for authenticating requests to OpenRouter's API
    • You can revoke or change your API key at any time through OpenRouter
  • Third-Party Service: OpenRouter is a third-party service. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy
  • User Consent: You must explicitly opt-in to use OpenRouter. The app will show a consent screen before using this provider

Important: When using OpenRouter, aggregated health metrics are sent over HTTPS to OpenRouter's servers for AI analysis. The analysis results are returned to your device and stored locally.

Data Transmission and Sharing

By Default: No Data Transmission

By default, your health data never leaves your device. We do NOT:

  • Transmit your health data to any servers (unless you explicitly opt-in to OpenRouter)
  • Sell your health data
  • Share your health data with third parties (except OpenRouter when you explicitly choose that provider)
  • Use your health data for advertising
  • Track you across apps or websites
  • Create advertising profiles based on your health data

When Using Cloud-Based AI (OpenRouter)

Only if you explicitly choose to use OpenRouter as your AI provider, aggregated health metrics (not raw data) are sent to OpenRouter's servers for processing. This requires your explicit opt-in consent.

User Control

You have complete control over your data:

  1. HealthKit Permissions: You can grant or revoke access to specific health metrics at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → VitalPath
  2. AI Provider Selection: You can choose which AI provider to use (or use Auto mode) in the app's Settings
  3. Data Export: You can export your health data as a formatted prompt for use with other AI services
  4. Data Deletion: You can delete the app at any time, which removes all locally stored data

Data Usage Transparency

The app includes a Data Usage Inspector that shows you exactly:

  • What health data was accessed
  • When it was accessed
  • Which AI provider was used
  • What data was sent (if using cloud-based providers)

Access this feature in Settings → Privacy & Data → Data Usage.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Keychain Storage: API keys are stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • HTTPS Encryption: All network communications use HTTPS encryption (only when using OpenRouter)
  • No Analytics Collection: We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics without your explicit consent
  • Local Processing: By default, all AI processing happens on your device—no data transmission
  • On-Device Storage: All data remains on your device unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud processing

Children's Privacy

VitalPath is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly access personal information from children under 13.
