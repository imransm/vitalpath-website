Support

Thank you for using VitalPath! We're here to help with any questions, issues, or feedback you may have.

Contact Us

For support, questions, or feedback, please reach out to us:

Email: vitalpathfeedback@gmail.com

How We Can Help

We can assist you with:

  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Questions about app features and functionality
  • Privacy and data usage inquiries
  • Bug reports and feature requests
  • General feedback about your experience

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. For urgent issues, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.

In-App Support

You can also access support directly from the VitalPath app:

  • Go to Settings tab
  • Use the Feedback feature to send us a message
  • Check the Data Usage Inspector for information about data access

Privacy Policy

For information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
